Call for Research Proposals

Ameliore Foundation is inviting researchers and documentary filmmakers to submit proposals for projects focusing on the environmental degradation in North Bihar, particularly in the agricultural sector. The selected proposal will be awarded a cash prize of INR 1,50,001.

Research Call 1: Midwifery Services in Rural Areas

Midwifery Services in Rural Areas: We have observed a significant gap in the availability of midwifery services in rural areas. Traditional services are dwindling, and government provisions are not reaching all sections of society. We are interested in research that explores: The current state of midwifery services in rural areas. The impact of this gap on women's health and childbirth outcomes. Potential solutions, such as training local women to serve as nurses in their communities.

Research Call 2: Education and Learning Opportunities

Education facilities are lacking in rural areas, particularly for students up to class 12. We are interested in research that explores: The current state of education in rural areas, particularly in the Basopatti Block of Madhubani district. The impact of this lack of facilities on student outcomes. Potential solutions, such as remote learning or coaching programs.

Research Call 3: Modern Agricultural Practices

Small land farmers and cultivators in rural areas could benefit from the use of modern tools, equipment, and soil testing facilities. We are interested in research that explores: The current state of agricultural practices in rural areas. The impact of these practices on crop yields and farmer income. Potential solutions, such as rental programs for modern farming equipment and soil testing facilities.
