About Us

Ameliore Foundation was established with a vision to create a sustainable and inclusive world. Our team comprises of dedicated individuals who are passionate about making a difference. Over the years, we have initiated and successfully executed numerous projects that have positively impacted the lives of many. Our work is driven by our mission to improve the quality of life and environment. We are proud of the difference we have made and are committed to continue our work. Our Work

Education

Education is a powerful tool for change. At Ameliore Foundation, we offer various educational initiatives including internship programmes, literature promotion, extended travel events, management consultancy, United Nations liaisoning, and policy advocacy. Our educational initiatives are designed to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to contribute to their communities and the world.
